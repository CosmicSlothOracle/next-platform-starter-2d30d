Who we are

We are a group of people who share the common desire to make healthcare in the neighborhood more holistic and social. We have been working as the Collective for Solidarity Health e.V. to implement a neighborhood health center in Berlin. The Concept

The concept

Body and soul, where and how we live, and our place in society collectively influence our health and quality of life. Viewing and strengthening these as a whole is a crucial aspect of our neighborhood health center idea. Specifically, this means close collaboration between various professional groups within healthcare (such as doctors from different specialties, psychologists, therapists, nursing professionals) as well as social workers and people active in legal, educational, and activist fields. It is particularly important to us that neighborhood residents can actively participate in the development and design of the center, creating a place that is adapted to their needs and grows with them.

Our Goal

Our goal

Our goal is to promote health - that is, the physical, mental, and social well-being - of neighborhood residents and offer a place of encounter, exchange, and networking (e.g., in the form of a caf√©, spaces for workshops and groups). Currently, our work focuses on building initial care offerings in Lichtenberg, but we aim to bring the neighborhood health center concept to all Berlin neighborhoods where residents can be particularly empowered.
